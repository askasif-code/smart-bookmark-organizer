Privacy Policy for Smart Bookmark Organizer

Last Updated: October 29, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Smart Bookmark Organizer stores all data locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

2. Data Storage

All bookmarks, folders, tags, and settings are stored locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local API. This data never leaves your device.

3. Permissions Used

  • storage: To save your bookmarks locally
  • tabs: To detect current page information
  • activeTab: To extract metadata from pages you bookmark

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT share, sell, or transmit your data to any third parties. Your bookmarks remain completely private and local to your device.

5. Data Export

You can export your bookmarks as JSON or CSV files at any time. This is optional and controlled by you.

6. Contact

For questions or concerns, contact: askasif.developer@gmail.com

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected in the extension listing.
